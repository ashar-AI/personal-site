## Why Speaking-First Matters

Traditional apps left me fluent in tests but silent in real Tokyo conversations. SocioLingo fixes this through:

- **Real-World Video Context**: Learn phrases actually used in konbini, izakayas, and train stations
- **No More Robot Voices**: AI tutors with natural Japanese speech patterns
- **Mistake-Driven Learning**: Focus practice where you struggle most

## AI Core Features

### 1. Conversation Simulator
Practice realistic scenarios with AI that:
- Responds to your actual Japanese input
- Throws cultural curveballs ("What if the cashier gives wrong change?")
- Adapts difficulty based on your level

### 2. Precision Feedback System 
Our AI analyzes in 3 dimensions:
- **Pronunciation** (pitch accent detection)
- **Grammar** (natural particle usage)
- **Cultural Appropriateness** (honorifics checker)

### Natural Voice Synthesis
